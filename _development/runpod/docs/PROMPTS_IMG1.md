# Prompts para img1 (soldados lutando na neve)

> Estilo baseado no P2_cinema que teve melhor resultado
> Padrao: camera → acao com verbo forte → detalhe fisico → ambiente → som → estilo

## Negative prompt (usar em TODOS):
```
pc game, console game, video game, cartoon, childish, ugly, blurry, distorted, watermark, text, logo, anime, CGI, 3D render, low quality
```

## P1 — Impacto fisico
```
Handheld camera, two soldiers throwing heavy punches at each other in the snow. Fists connecting with faces, bodies stumbling backward from impact. Snow bursting from their boots. Destroyed buildings burning behind them, black smoke rising. Sounds of struggle and distant gunfire. 35mm war film footage, gritty and raw.
```

## P2 — Grappling
```
Shaky close-up camera, two soldiers grabbing each other by the collar and wrestling violently through the snow. One forces the other down, the second pushes back with all his strength. Heavy breathing visible in the cold air. Fire crackling from ruins behind them. Cinematic war documentary, desaturated colors.
```

## P3 — Movimento dinamico
```
Tracking camera following two soldiers charging at each other and colliding in brutal combat. They spin and shove through deep snow, kicking up white powder. Tank turret rotating slowly in the background. Snowfall intensifying. Artillery rumbling in the distance. Raw wartime footage, 35mm film grain.
```

## P4 — Cinematografico
```
Slow push-in camera, two exhausted soldiers trading blows in the middle of a snow-covered battlefield. Each punch lands with weight and realism. Embers drifting through the air from burning buildings. Thick smoke and falling snow. Epic cinematic war scene, dramatic lighting, film grain.
```

## P5 — Curto e direto
```
Two soldiers fighting violently in the snow, punching and grappling, camera push in, medium close-up, burning ruins in background, heavy snowfall, sounds of combat and explosions, cinematic war footage.
```
